Max Rive

Max Rive is a professional photographer born in Netherlands. Max passion for mountains and landscapes began when he would visit high mountains of Switzerland at the age of 5. Max planed a last-minute hike trip is Switzerland and ended up in hospital for 3 days, he found his passion for mountings again and returned many times. Max other hobbies were motorsport and cycling, but he had little interest and motivation for his studies. Max would travel while he was studying and was highly motivated by his environment, he missed his deadline for his studies and later drop out to travel around the world almost losing his life in an airplane accident to the Himalayas of Nepal.Max heard the news when he was in shooting Greenland that he won the International Landscape Photographer (IPA) awards in 2017 one of the biggest awards in todayâ€™s Landscape Photography. Max ambition is to win the IPA and his dream has come true. He believes in hard work and anything is possible.

The video above of Max Rive of why he become a photographer and the experience he had from shooting photos of mountains. He explained when shooting at the same locations each time he will have different experience because each experience was not the same. At the beginning of his journey, he prefer smaller compact cameras but eventually changing to a lot bigger cameras with different lens due to better quality images and professionalism. Smartphones have better pixels than smaller compact cameras which show how far technology has come. Max Rive has inspired me to want to become a more successful photographer myself. When shooting my images I have felt simillar experience as Rive about not every shot is the same.
